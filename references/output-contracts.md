# Output Contracts

Use concise Markdown. Include source provenance and limitations.

## AOI Screening Brief

1. Research question.
2. Normalized AOI.
3. Commodity and deposit model.
4. Data sources to use or evidence retrieved.
5. Geological and structural evidence.
6. Geochemical evidence.
7. Geophysical or remote sensing evidence if available.
8. Mineral occurrence evidence.
9. Prospectivity assessment with confidence.
10. Data gaps and next work program.
11. Limitations.
12. Sources and provenance.

## Geochemical Anomaly Interpretation

1. Dataset summary.
2. QA/QC review.
3. Background and threshold notes.
4. Pathfinder element associations.
5. Anomaly zones or values.
6. Deposit-model relevance.
7. Limitations.
8. Recommended follow-up.

## Mineral Occurrence And Deposit Model Memo

1. Research question.
2. Normalized AOI and jurisdiction.
3. Occurrence table.
4. Location confidence.
5. Commodity and model relevance.
6. Historic production or resource cautions.
7. Deposit-model fit.
8. Conflicts, gaps, and next verification steps.

## Data Gap And Next Work Program

1. Current evidence state.
2. Critical missing data.
3. Priority field or desktop work.
4. Expected decision impact.
5. Professional review needs.
6. Limitations.

## NI 43-101 Disclosure Risk Checklist

1. Scope and non-legal boundary.
2. Risk level.
3. Phrase or issue.
4. Why it matters.
5. Safer wording direction.
6. Required professional review.
7. Overall limitations.
