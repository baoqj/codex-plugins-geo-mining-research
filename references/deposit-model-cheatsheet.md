# Deposit Model Cheatsheet

Use this as a concise checklist. Model fit is not economic viability.

## Porphyry Cu-Mo-Au

- Setting: calc-alkaline to alkaline intrusions, arc or post-arc settings.
- Evidence: porphyritic intrusions, stockwork veining, potassic to phyllic alteration, Cu-Mo-Au anomalies, magnetic or IP response.
- Pathfinders: Cu, Mo, Au, Ag, Re, W, As, Sb, Bi, Te.
- Common gaps: intrusion age, alteration vectoring, sulphide abundance, geophysics, drilling.

## Orogenic Gold

- Setting: regional shear zones and greenstone or metamorphic belts.
- Evidence: quartz-carbonate veins, brittle-ductile structures, sulphides, alteration halos, Au with As-Sb-W-Bi-Te.
- Pathfinders: Au, As, Sb, W, Bi, Te, Ag.
- Common gaps: structural control, vein continuity, nugget effect, QA/QC.

## Volcanogenic Massive Sulphide

- Setting: submarine volcanic or volcano-sedimentary belts.
- Evidence: stratiform sulphides, felsic volcanic centers, alteration pipes, exhalites, EM conductors.
- Pathfinders: Cu, Zn, Pb, Ag, Au, Ba, Mn, Fe.
- Common gaps: stratigraphic position, conductor source, alteration footprint.

## LCT Pegmatite

- Setting: fertile peraluminous granite systems and pegmatite fields.
- Evidence: pegmatite dykes, spodumene or lepidolite, fractionation indicators, Li-Cs-Ta enrichment.
- Pathfinders: Li, Cs, Ta, Rb, Sn, Be, Nb, K.
- Common gaps: overburden, pegmatite mapping, mineralogy confirmation, representative sampling.

## Magmatic Ni-Cu-PGE

- Setting: mafic-ultramafic intrusions, conduits, komatiites, or layered intrusions.
- Evidence: sulphide-bearing mafic-ultramafic rocks, Ni-Cu-PGE geochemistry, EM conductors, gravity or magnetic context.
- Pathfinders: Ni, Cu, Co, Pt, Pd, Cr, Mg, S.
- Common gaps: sulphide tenor, contamination evidence, conductor source, metallurgy.

## IOCG

- Setting: crustal-scale structures and iron oxide alteration systems.
- Evidence: hematite or magnetite alteration, Cu-Au-U-REE associations, gravity and magnetic anomalies.
- Pathfinders: Cu, Au, U, REE, Co, Ag, F, Ba.
- Common gaps: alteration zoning, fluid pathway, age constraints, geophysical ambiguity.

## MVT

- Setting: carbonate platforms and basin-margin structures.
- Evidence: stratabound Pb-Zn sulphides, dolomitization, breccias, basin brines.
- Pathfinders: Pb, Zn, Ag, Ba, Sr, Cd.
- Common gaps: host stratigraphy, fluid pathway, sulphide continuity.

## Skarn

- Setting: intrusive contacts with carbonate or reactive host rocks.
- Evidence: garnet-pyroxene alteration, magnetite or sulphide zones, Cu-Au-W-Zn-Pb-Mo signatures.
- Pathfinders: Cu, Au, W, Zn, Pb, Mo, Bi, Te, Fe.
- Common gaps: intrusive relation, alteration zonation, metal zoning, depth continuity.
