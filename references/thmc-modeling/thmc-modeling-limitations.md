# THMC Modeling Limitations

Common limitations:

- Lack of site-specific parameters.
- Uncertainty in thermodynamic databases.
- Kinetic rate uncertainty.
- Scale mismatch between laboratory and field systems.
- Fracture network simplification.
- Equilibrium versus kinetic assumptions.
- Sparse monitoring data.
- Long-term extrapolation uncertainty.
- Non-unique calibration.
- Computational instability.
- Simplified boundary conditions.
- Uncertain source terms.

Report these limitations explicitly and distinguish concept models from validated simulations.
