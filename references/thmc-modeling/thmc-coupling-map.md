# THMC Coupling Map

- T -> H: temperature affects viscosity, density, convection, and hydraulic conductivity.
- T -> C: temperature affects equilibrium constants, kinetic rates, diffusion, and solubility.
- T -> M: thermal expansion and thermal stress.
- H -> T: groundwater advection transports heat.
- H -> M: pore pressure affects effective stress.
- H -> C: advection, dispersion, and diffusion transport species.
- M -> H: porosity, permeability, fracture aperture, and damage alter flow.
- M -> C: fracture opening/closure changes reactive surface area and mineral exposure.
- C -> H: precipitation and dissolution change porosity and permeability.
- C -> M: cementation, dissolution weakening, swelling, and mineral transformation affect mechanics.
- C -> T: reaction heat is usually optional or negligible unless justified.

Mark every coupling as required, optional, negligible, or excluded.
