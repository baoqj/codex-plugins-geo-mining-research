# THMC Publication Caveats

Required caveats for THMC outputs:

- This is a conceptual modeling or planning output, not a validated numerical simulation unless solver runs and calibration are documented.
- It is not engineering certification.
- It is not environmental legal advice.
- It is not a substitute for laboratory or field calibration.
- It is not a permitting, compliance, feasibility, investment, resource, or reserve conclusion.
- Professional hydrogeology, geochemistry, geomechanics, engineering, and regulatory review may be required before use.
