# Stress, Permeability, and Porosity Laws

Use mechanical feedback only when the research question needs it.

Common options:

- Effective stress relation.
- Stress-dependent permeability.
- Porosity update from volumetric strain.
- Kozeny-Carman style permeability-porosity relation.
- Mineral precipitation clogging.
- Mineral dissolution opening.
- Damage-dependent permeability.

Required data: stress state, pore pressure, rock stiffness, fracture stiffness, porosity, permeability, swelling or compaction behavior.
