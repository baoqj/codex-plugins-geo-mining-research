# THMC Coupling Matrix

| From / To | Thermal | Hydrological | Mechanical | Chemical |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Thermal | - | T affects viscosity, density, and convection | thermal expansion or thermal stress | T affects equilibrium constants, kinetics, diffusion, and solubility |
| Hydrological | advective heat transport | - | pore pressure affects effective stress | advection, dispersion, and diffusion transport species |
| Mechanical | deformation heat usually minor | stress changes porosity, permeability, and fracture aperture | - | deformation exposes surfaces or closes pathways |
| Chemical | reaction heat usually optional | precipitation/dissolution changes porosity and permeability | cementation, dissolution weakening, swelling, or clogging | - |

## Notes

- Mark each coupling as required, optional, negligible, or excluded.
- Do not add coupling terms without evidence or a research reason.
