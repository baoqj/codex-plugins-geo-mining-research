# PINN / PyTorch Surrogate Route

Use only as a surrogate, inversion, or acceleration layer after a physics-based model or benchmark exists.

Strengths:

- Can support inverse modeling and fast scenario screening.
- Integrates well with AI workflows.

Limitations:

- Must be validated against physical solvers and data.
- Not a replacement for mass balance, thermodynamic consistency, or calibrated THMC simulation.
