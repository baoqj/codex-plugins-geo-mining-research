# Variable and Parameter Glossary

| Symbol | Field | Meaning | Unit |
|---|---|---|---|
| T | Thermal | temperature | K or deg C |
| p | Hydrological | pressure | Pa |
| h | Hydrological | hydraulic head | m |
| q | Hydrological | Darcy flux | m/s |
| S_w | Hydrological | water saturation | dimensionless |
| C_i | Chemical | concentration of species i | mol/m3 |
| SI | Chemical | saturation index | dimensionless |
| phi | H/M/C | porosity | dimensionless |
| k | H/M/C | permeability | m2 |
| D | H/C | dispersion/diffusion tensor | m2/s |
| u | Mechanical | displacement | m |
| sigma | Mechanical | stress | Pa |
| epsilon | Mechanical | strain | dimensionless |
| alpha | Mechanical | Biot coefficient | dimensionless |
| R_i | Chemical | reaction source/sink | mol/m3/s |
