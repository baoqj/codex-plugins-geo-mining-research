# Thermal Transport and Temperature Effects

Thermal effects may include:

- Heat conduction.
- Advective heat transport by groundwater.
- Geothermal gradient.
- Waste heat or mining heat pollution.
- Temperature-dependent viscosity and density.
- Temperature-dependent diffusion.
- Temperature-dependent equilibrium constants.
- Temperature-dependent kinetic rates.
- Thermal expansion or thermal stress when M is active.

Exclude thermal coupling when temperature is constant or not material to the research question.
