- Master elements: H, O, C, Ca, Mg, Na, K, Cl, S, Fe, Mn, U, Ra, Rn, Pb, Po.
- Uranium redox: U(VI)/U(IV).
- Carbonate complexation for U(VI).
- Sorption on Fe/Mn oxides, clays, and organic matter if supported.
- Ra control by barite or co-precipitation where supported.
- Rn gas phase behavior where relevant.
- Pb/Po ingrowth and sorption.
- Decay-chain ingrowth and half-life data, only from verified sources.

Required data: pH, Eh, alkalinity, major ions, radionuclide concentrations, mineralogy, sorption constraints, source term, water residence time.
