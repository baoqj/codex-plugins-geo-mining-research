# Uncertainty Sensitivity Skill

## Purpose

Design sensitivity and uncertainty analysis for THMC models and connect the results to research conclusions or decision relevance.

## Methods

- One-at-a-time local sensitivity.
- Latin Hypercube sampling.
- Monte Carlo simulation.
- Sobol or variance-based indices.
- Scenario comparison.
- Structural model comparison.
- Long-term uncertainty envelope.

## Output Contract

Return:

- Key uncertain parameters.
- Sensitivity design.
- Uncertainty propagation plan.
- Recommended visualizations.
- Decision relevance.
- Long-term extrapolation caveats.
