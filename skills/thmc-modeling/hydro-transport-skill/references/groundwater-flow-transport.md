# Groundwater Flow and Transport

Core H model decisions:

- Saturated versus unsaturated flow.
- Equivalent porous medium versus discrete fractures.
- Steady versus transient flow.
- Conservative transport versus reactive transport.
- Advection, dispersion, diffusion, and retardation.

Required data:

- Hydraulic conductivity.
- Porosity.
- Storage or specific yield.
- Boundary heads or fluxes.
- Recharge and discharge.
- Dispersivity and diffusion coefficient.
- Observation heads and concentrations.
