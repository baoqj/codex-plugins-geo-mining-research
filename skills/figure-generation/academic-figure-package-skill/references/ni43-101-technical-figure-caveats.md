# NI 43-101 Technical Figure Caveats

Figures for Canadian mining disclosure must not imply:

- A Qualified Person opinion.
- A current mineral resource or reserve.
- Feasibility, profitability, mineability, or investment suitability.
- Verified metallurgy, permitting, environmental baseline, or Indigenous consultation status.

Use captions and caveats to state whether content is conceptual, historical, unverified, inferred, or based on supplied evidence only. Require QP and legal review for public disclosure.
