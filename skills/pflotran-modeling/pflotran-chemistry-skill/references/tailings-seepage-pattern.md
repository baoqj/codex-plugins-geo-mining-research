# Tailings Seepage Pattern

Use for tailings porewater entering shallow groundwater.

Model components:

- source zone in tailings or basal seepage;
- shallow aquifer receptor;
- sulfate, acidity, metals, alkalinity, and carbonate buffering;
- Fe/Al hydroxide precipitation and metal attenuation;
- optional variably saturated flow in tailings;
- observation points along downgradient flow path.

Do not infer plume extent, flux, or compliance without field hydrogeology and monitoring data.
