# Uranium Reactive Transport Pattern

Use for carbonate-rich groundwater, U-series radionuclide transport, fractured granitic groundwater, and DGR-style screening.

Key processes:

- uranium carbonate/hydroxide complexation;
- redox sensitivity of U(IV)/U(VI);
- Ra attenuation by sulfate/barite or sorption;
- Fe/Mn oxide surface interaction;
- mineral precipitation/dissolution controls;
- long-term spatial transport and breakthrough.

Route single-sample speciation or saturation-index screening to PHREEQC before PFLOTRAN.
