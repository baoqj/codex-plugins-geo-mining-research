# Sorption And Ion Exchange Patterns

Use sorption or ion exchange only when the model objective requires attenuation/retardation and site/lab data or documented constants exist.

Required data:

- sorbent mineral or surface type;
- site density or exchange capacity;
- surface area or solid mass;
- compatible constants and database;
- pH/redox/ionic-strength constraints;
- calibration/validation observations.

Do not infer attenuation capacity from mineral presence alone.
