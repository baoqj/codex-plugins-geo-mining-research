# Mineral Reaction Patterns

Common PFLOTRAN mineral patterns:

- carbonate buffering: calcite, dolomite, siderite;
- sulfate minerals: gypsum, barite, celestite;
- Fe/Al hydroxides: ferrihydrite, goethite, gibbsite;
- sulfides: pyrite, pyrrhotite, sphalerite, chalcopyrite, galena;
- silicates/clays: quartz, feldspar, chlorite, illite, smectite.

For each mineral, require initial volume fraction or reactive surface area, reaction law/equilibrium assumption, and thermodynamic source.
