## Model Limitations

- The input deck is a skeleton unless executed and debugged.
- Boundary conditions, porosity, permeability, dispersivity, diffusion coefficients, thermal properties, reaction constants, and mineral volume fractions must come from measured data or documented sources.
- Thermodynamic and kinetic database coverage controls the interpretation.
- Redox disequilibrium, colloids, microbial effects, fracture heterogeneity, and scale effects may not be represented.
- Calibration and validation are required before quantitative prediction or regulatory interpretation.
