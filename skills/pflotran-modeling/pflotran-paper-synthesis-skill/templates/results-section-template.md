## Results Interpretation Plan

No PFLOTRAN results should be reported unless a solver run was executed and output files are available. When outputs are available, interpret:

- hydraulic heads, pressure, saturation, and fluxes;
- concentration fields and breakthrough curves;
- pH, alkalinity, redox proxies, and major-ion evolution;
- mineral volume fractions and reaction-front locations;
- porosity/permeability changes where enabled;
- mass-balance and convergence diagnostics.

Separate calculated outputs from field measurements and state calibration/validation status explicitly.
