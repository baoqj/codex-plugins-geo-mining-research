# Geomechanics Scope And Limits

PFLOTRAN-oriented workflows can discuss porosity/permeability feedback from reactions and pressure/thermal assumptions, but full nonlinear geomechanics may require OpenGeoSys, COMSOL, or another THM/THMC solver.

Escalate solver comparison when the science depends on deformation, fracture aperture evolution, damage, swelling, compaction, or stress redistribution.
