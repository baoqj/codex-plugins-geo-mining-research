# Porosity And Permeability Feedback

Porosity/permeability feedback may arise from mineral dissolution/precipitation, clogging, cementation, or fracture filling.

Required data:

- initial porosity/permeability;
- mineral volume fractions;
- reaction stoichiometry and molar volumes;
- empirical permeability-porosity relationship;
- calibration observations.

Do not invent feedback laws or claim permeability evolution without model execution and validation.
