# Geochemistry Paper Architecture Quality Checklist

## Scientific Question

- [ ] The paper has a specific scientific problem.
- [ ] The knowledge gap is explicit.
- [ ] Research questions are answerable with the planned data.
- [ ] Hypotheses are testable or clearly marked as conceptual.

## Data

- [ ] Data sources, versions, and provenance are listed.
- [ ] Sample metadata requirements are defined.
- [ ] Units and coordinate systems are standardized.
- [ ] Analytical methods and detection limits are required.
- [ ] Missing values, censored data, and outliers have a policy.
- [ ] QA/QC requirements are explicit.

## Methods

- [ ] Formulas, constants, and classification schemes require citations.
- [ ] Software, version, and database requirements are listed.
- [ ] Model parameters and boundary conditions are placeholders if unavailable.
- [ ] Uncertainty treatment is planned.

## Figures and Tables

- [ ] Every figure supports a specific paper claim.
- [ ] Axes, units, legends, captions, and sources are required.
- [ ] Maps require CRS, scale, north arrow, source, and spatial precision.
- [ ] Figure captions can stand alone.
- [ ] Tables include source/provenance and units where relevant.

## Discussion

- [ ] Mechanisms are supported by evidence.
- [ ] Alternative explanations are listed.
- [ ] Prior work comparison is planned.
- [ ] Boundary conditions are stated.
- [ ] Correlation is not written as causation.

## Conclusions

- [ ] Conclusions only summarize results already presented.
- [ ] Each conclusion traces to evidence.
- [ ] Extrapolation is limited.
- [ ] Future work is specific and justified.
