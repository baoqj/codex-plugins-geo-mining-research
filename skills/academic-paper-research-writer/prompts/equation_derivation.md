- reaction-diffusion equations;
- Nernst-Planck transport;
- Poisson equation for electric potential;
- Onsager recombination radius;
- field-modified recombination probability;
- surface reaction terms;
- mass balance for H2 and H2O2;
- energy balance;
- efficiency metrics;
- scale-up constraints.

Rules:
- Do not include undefined symbols.
- Do not use equations as decoration.
- Every equation must serve a specific argument.
- If a formula is approximate, state the approximation.
- If a parameter is unknown, identify how it should be measured or estimated.
